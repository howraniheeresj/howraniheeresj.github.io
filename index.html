RESC

Resilient Equitable Sustainable Communities Consulting

At RESC Consulting, we believe that all people deserve to live in neighborhoods that are resilient to the impact of climate change like flooding, high heat, and storms. All people should be able to access the benefits of sustainability investments like clean air, quality local greenspace, and affordable and comfortable homes and businesses. However, too often, these benefits are disproportionately distributed and communities of color are often denied their share of resilience and sustainability benefits. RESC offers the following services to enable local governments, non profits, and small businesses help achieve these benefits for their residents, stakeholders, and customers:

  • Strategic planning
  • Group facilitation
  • Stakeholder engagement
  • Grant scoping and writing
  • Racial equity training
  • Pilot program development
  • Program implementation
  • Project management
  • Data visualization and dashboarding
